Cookies Policy

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the website owners. Cookies help us improve your experience when using our Service by remembering your preferences, understanding how you use our website, and tailoring content and offers to your interests.

Cookies can be "persistent" cookies (which remain on your device for a set period of time) or "session" cookies (which are deleted when you close your browser).

2. How We Use Cookies

When you access and use our Service, we may place cookies on your device for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the basic operation of our website. They enable core functionality such as user authentication, account login, shopping cart management, and secure checkout. Without these cookies, the Service cannot function properly.
  • Performance & Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. We use this data to improve the performance and usability of our Service, identify technical issues, and optimize our website.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies enable us to remember your preferences and choices (such as your language preference, login details, and region) to provide a more personalized and convenient experience on our website.
  • Marketing & Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and marketing content to you. They may be used by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant ads on other websites. They also help us measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

The table below details the types of cookies we may use on our Service:

4. Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use third-party cookies from trusted service providers to help us operate and improve our Service. These third parties include:

  • Analytics providers (such as Google Analytics) to help us understand how users interact with our website

These third parties may access your cookie data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We have no control over third-party cookies, and we recommend that you review the privacy and cookie policies of any third-party websites you visit.

5. How to Manage or Disable Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can choose to block cookies, delete existing cookies, or receive a notification before a cookie is placed on your device. Please note that if you choose to disable or block cookies, some parts of our Service may not function properly, as essential cookies are required for the core functionality of our website.

You can manage your cookie preferences through the following links for the most common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

For mobile devices, you can manage cookie settings in the browser app's settings menu on your smartphone or tablet.

We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our use of cookies.
