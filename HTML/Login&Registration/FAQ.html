Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Login

How do I create a DragonGod Phone account?
You can create an account by clicking the "Register" button on the login page. You will need to provide a valid email address or phone number, set a secure password, and complete the verification process. Once registered, you can use your credentials to log in to our platform.
I forgot my password. How can I reset it?
Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You will need to enter your registered email address or phone number to receive a verification code. After entering the correct code, you can set a new password for your account.
Can I use multiple login methods for the same account?
Yes. You can log in to your account via password, SMS verification code, or QR code scan (using the DragonGod Phone app). All login methods are linked to your registered account.
How do I secure my account?
We recommend using a strong, unique password for your account, enabling two-factor authentication (if available), and never sharing your login credentials with third parties. You should also regularly check your account activity and notify us immediately of any suspicious login attempts.

Orders & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers, and selected digital payment methods. All payments are processed via secure, encrypted payment gateways.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, we will send a tracking number to your registered email address. You can also track your order status in the "My Orders" section of your account on our website or app.
Can I cancel or modify my order after placing it?
You can cancel or modify your order within 24 hours of placement, provided the order has not yet been processed for shipping. To request a cancellation or modification, please contact our customer service team immediately. Once the order has been shipped, you will need to follow our return process.

Products & Delivery

Are all products sold on DragonGod Phone authentic and brand new?
Yes. All products sold on our platform are 100% authentic, brand new, and sealed in original packaging. We source all products directly from official manufacturers and authorized distributors, and all products come with the official manufacturer warranty.
What is the delivery time for my order?
Standard delivery typically takes 3-7 business days for domestic orders, and 7-15 business days for international orders. Express delivery options are also available at checkout for faster delivery. Delivery times may vary due to your location, customs processing, or unforeseen shipping delays.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries and regions. Shipping costs and delivery times will be calculated at checkout based on your delivery address. Please note that international orders may be subject to import duties, taxes, or customs fees, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

After-Sales & Returns

What is your return and refund policy?
We offer a 7-day no-reason return policy for unopened, unused products in their original packaging, and a 30-day return policy for defective products. For products with quality issues covered by the manufacturer warranty, we will arrange a free repair, replacement, or refund according to the warranty terms.
How do I initiate a return?
To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team within the eligible return period, providing your order number and details of the issue. Our team will review your request and guide you through the return process. Once we receive and inspect the returned product, we will process your refund or replacement.
How long does it take to process a refund?
Once we receive and approve your returned product, we will process the refund within 3-5 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment provider, typically 5-10 business days.
