Account & Login Help

Can't log in to my account

If you are unable to log in, please try the following solutions:

  1. Check that your username/email/phone number and password are entered correctly, and that Caps Lock is not enabled
  2. Reset your password by clicking "Forgot Password" on the login page
  3. Clear your browser cache and cookies, or try logging in with a different browser/device
  4. If your account is locked, please contact customer service for assistance

How to register a new account

Click the "Register" button on the login page, enter your valid email/phone number, complete the verification, set a password, and you can complete the registration. You need to be at least 18 years old to register an account.

How to change my account password

After logging in to your account, go to "Account Settings" - "Security Settings" to change your password. If you can't log in, you can reset your password via the "Forgot Password" function on the login page.

Orders & Payment Help

How to check my order status

After logging in to your account, go to the "My Orders" page to view all your order details, shipping status, tracking numbers and other information. We will also send order update notifications to your registered email.

What payment methods are supported

We support all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers, and mainstream digital payment methods. All payments are processed through encrypted secure channels, and we will not store your complete payment information.

Can I cancel or modify my order

You can apply for order cancellation or modification within 24 hours of placing the order (before the order is shipped). Please contact customer service as soon as possible to process your request. Once the order has been shipped, you will need to apply for a return after receiving the goods.

Shipping & Product Help

Delivery time and shipping range

Domestic standard delivery takes 3-7 working days, and international delivery takes 7-15 working days. We support shipping to most countries and regions around the world. The specific shipping cost and delivery time will be calculated at checkout according to your address.

How to track my package

After your order is shipped, we will send a shipping notification email with the tracking number and logistics query link. You can also check the real-time tracking information of the package in the order details page.

Are all products authentic and have a warranty

All products sold on the platform are 100% brand new and authentic, sourced directly from official manufacturers and authorized distributors. All mobile phones come with a standard 12-month manufacturer's warranty, and accessories come with a 6-month warranty.

After-sales & Returns Help

Return and exchange policy

We offer a 7-day no-reason return policy for unopened products in original packaging, and a 30-day free return/exchange policy for products with quality issues. For products within the warranty period, we provide free repair services according to the warranty terms.

View full after-sales policy

How to apply for a return

To apply for a return, please contact our customer service team within the eligible return period, provide your order number and the reason for the return. After the application is approved, we will guide you to return the product, and process the refund or exchange after receiving and inspecting the product.

How long does it take to process a refund

After we receive and confirm the returned product, the refund will be processed within 3-5 working days. The time for the refund to arrive in your account depends on your payment institution, usually 5-10 working days.
